*This article may be updated as new information or corrections become available.*

On Friday evening, a school bus with students from Woodnville High's marching band experienced a brake failure and skidded before stopping in a driveway. **There were no injuries** and no significant damage to the bus or any property.

The bus, No. 143, left Pop Keeney Stadium after Woodinville's football game against Bothell earlier in the day, and was on the way back to Woodnville High School when one of its tires blew out.

"On the way back to [Woodnville] from Pop Keeney, the bus had a tyre blow out, the pressure cut the brake lines and we sorta just skidded around until we took a sharp turn into some guy's driveway and stopped," a member of the marching band wrote on Discord. In a picture taken after the incident by the same person, ambulances could be seen on site.

The precise extent of the damage, as well as whether buses on Monday may be impacted, is not yet known. There is no known damage to bus 143 besides the blown tire and inoperative brakes.
